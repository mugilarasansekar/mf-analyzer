Understanding these metrics

Max Drawdown is the largest peak-to-trough decline before a new high was set. It represents the worst loss a buy-and-hold investor could have experienced.

Recovery cycle is the longest time the fund took to return to a previous peak — a measure of how long you'd be "underwater."

Up days ratio is the percentage of trading days where the NAV closed higher than the previous day.
